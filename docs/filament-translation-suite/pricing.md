## License Terms

### Project-Based Licensing

- **Solo** — 1 project. A single application — however many domains, servers, or environments you need to run it.
- **Agency** — Unlimited projects

Local development, staging, and CI environments are **unlimited** and do not count toward your project limit.

### Lifetime Access, Update-Renewable

This is the part that matters:

- **You buy once, you own it forever.** Every version you download is yours to keep and use indefinitely. No subscription trap. No "your license expired" popups in production.
- **1 year of updates** — all minor and major versions released during your active update period
- **Email support** — questions, bug reports, and feature requests

After your update period ends, you can continue using the last version you downloaded for as long as you want. Your code doesn't stop working. You just won't receive new versions or support until you renew.

**Renewal is $39 (Solo) or $69 (Agency)** — a fraction of the initial price — to re-activate another full year of updates and support.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try it before buying?

We don't offer a free trial, but we do offer a **14-day money-back guarantee**. If the suite doesn't meet your needs, contact us for a full refund — no questions asked.

### What counts as a "project"?

A project is a single application or product. If you build `client-a.com` and `client-b.com` as two separate Laravel apps, that's two projects. If you run `api.example.com` and `app.example.com` as the same Laravel app, that's one project.

### Can I upgrade from Solo to Agency?

Yes. Contact us and we'll credit your existing license toward an Agency plan.

### Do I need to pay for translation APIs separately?

Yes. The suite integrates with DeepL, Google Cloud Translate, OpenAI, and Anthropic, but you need your own API keys and account with those providers. We don't markup API costs.

### What happens if I don't renew?

Nothing breaks. Your existing installations continue working with the last version you downloaded. You keep lifetime access to that version. You just won't receive new updates or support until you renew.

### Why not a subscription?

Because we don't believe in holding your code hostage. You paid for the tool — you should be able to use it. The renewal model lets us keep improving the suite sustainably while giving you complete freedom to choose when (or if) you want the latest updates.
